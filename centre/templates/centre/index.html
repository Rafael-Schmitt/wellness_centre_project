Our Sanctuary

Located in the heart of Santa Catarina's tropical coastline, we blend ancient wellness traditions with the natural beauty of Brazil's Atlantic coast.

A Peaceful Haven Inspired by Nature

Our centre is designed to harmonize with the surrounding rainforest and ocean. Every space invites connection with the natural elements, from our open-air yoga pavilion to our meditation gardens overlooking the sea.

We offer personalized wellness programs that combine traditional Brazilian healing practices with modern wellness techniques, all delivered by experienced practitioners who honor the mind-body-spirit connection.

The rhythm of the ocean waves, the whisper of palm trees, and the vibrant energy of the rainforest create the perfect environment for transformation and renewal.

Wellness Experiences

Our holistic offerings are designed to nurture every aspect of your being, helping you find balance and inner peace.

Oceanfront Yoga

Begin your day with sunrise yoga sessions on our private beach, listening to the rhythm of the waves as you flow through mindful movements.

Rainforest Spa Treatments

Indulge in rejuvenating treatments using local ingredients like açai, coconut, and Amazonian clay, all while surrounded by the sounds of nature.

Guided Nature Immersion

Explore the Atlantic rainforest with our expert guides, learning about native plants and their healing properties on mindful walking meditations.

Plant-Based Brazilian Cuisine

Savor nourishing meals prepared with organic local produce, celebrating the vibrant flavors and nutritional benefits of Brazilian superfoods.

Sound Healing Under the Stars

Experience transformative sound baths using crystal bowls and traditional instruments, held in our open-air temple beneath the Southern Hemisphere stars.

Personal Wellness Coaching

Work one-on-one with our wellness practitioners to create a personalized plan for sustainable health and balance that you can take home with you.
